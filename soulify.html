Soulify is a new kind of immersive storytelling platform featuring an in-game marketplace for independent artists. Soulify is a single player, touch based experience targeted at mobile (VR compatible). Soulify will provide creator tools to customise avatars, digital fashion, environments and build worlds. We aim to form a community who share a deep love of creative expression, personal development, diversity, and most importantly, want to help others feel like they belong by sharing their stories. Connecting a global creative community directly to gamers, Soulify will build technology to enable digital goods to be shared by independent artists who typically lack access to existing in-game sales. Digital goods available in Soulify include digital fashion, works of art, graphics, music, videos, decor, and environments created by independent artists and designers. They enter the marketplace as a result of winning Soulify design contests which are voted on by the public. Inspirit360 handles the production of the game and gives designers a 25% cut of the in-game sales, in addition to a cash prize for winning the contest. The idea for Soulify originated from the lack of representation of women and marginalized groups in game design, and the negative effect this can have on the feeling of being positively embodied. "In self-objectification, girls internalise an observer’s perspective on their physical selves and learn to treat themselves as objects to be looked at and evaluated for their appearance” (Zurbriggen, 2007). In 2020, the gaming industry was valued at $179.7 billion (Witowski, 2021), and is becoming a huge, if rather unrecognised, influencer of culture (Wittenbeg-Cox, 2020). Women need improved representation in both games and in ads for games (Reach3, 2021). Soulify will be a free to play gaming environment where women and girls can thrive - a beautiful and sacred safe space where their unique appearance, heritage, and story is celebrated through the characters they meet and the community they become part of.
