About Inspirit360

Inspirit360 will run several apps, including digital storytelling platforms, games with marketplaces, community-related sites to interact with independent artists and designers, VR experiences, and cross platform avatar services.

Soulify is a design marketplace that connects users with artists to help them create something one-of-a-kind. The design contests are the core of the company and with millions of votes cast, some will count more than others. After years of dissecting data, Inspirit360 will know what kinds of voters are likely to be better at predicting bestsellers. We develop our own, best-in-class technology to connect designers and the public to build a moat around our business.

As contests draw in more designers, Inspirit360 will expand beyond Soulify and into physical retail, other brands and digital worlds to find new ways to use more of the designs (and new income sources for its artists, too). Soulify’s wholesale lines sold in stores, like activewear and future licensing deals around art, are growing to make up a large portion of the company’s revenue, with the rest from its in-game micro-transactions and optional premium subscriptions.

Soulify has a cosmopolitan culture which leads to a sharing of cultures and values, collective participation in events and increased understanding between people of different backgrounds and body shapes. The social impact of our project has increased a sense of belonging, and results in our users experiencing increased feelings of positive embodiment. Core to our growth will be our commitment to people, planet and profit and we would like to have achieved B Corp status for Inspirit360 by the end of our third year.

Soulify is a strong, well-known brand attracting licensing deals for tv shows, films, merchandise and toys. Soulify's model will lead to deals with brands like Nike and Vans, selling digital goods in some cases accompanied by physical products. Soulify is a digital world that is now able to transition into disrupting things being sold in physical retail. We’re a community of women designing products for women. We have proved there’s power in that.
